Japanese fiction

Basically japanese fiction can either be read or watched

  • Manga (reading)
  • anime (watching)

Haikyuu!

"Haikyuu" is a thrilling sports anime that follows the journey of Shoyo Hinata, a determined and vertically challenged volleyball enthusiast. Inspired by a legendary player known as "The Little Giant," Hinata joins his high school team and embarks on a quest to conquer the court. Filled with intense matches, fierce rivalries, and the power of teamwork, "Haikyuu" delivers an adrenaline-fueled story of passion, friendship, and the pursuit of victory.

Blue Lock

"Blue Lock" is a captivating anime centered around the ruthless world of soccer. Talented striker Yoichi Isagi finds himself trapped in a specialized training facility called Blue Lock, where he must compete against other promising players to earn a spot on the national team. With high stakes, intense rivalries, and strategic mind games, "Blue Lock" promises an exhilarating journey of ambition, skill, and the relentless pursuit of greatness.

Slam Dunk

Slam Dunk" is a classic sports anime that revolves around the journey of Hanamichi Sakuragi, a delinquent-turned-basketball player. Driven by his newfound love for the sport and fueled by his determination to impress a girl, Sakuragi joins the Shohoku High School basketball team. With intense matches, comedic moments, and personal growth, "Slam Dunk" showcases the power of teamwork, friendship, and the pursuit of excellence on the court.

American Fiction

The Natural

The Natural by Bernard Malamud: This novel tells the story of Roy Hobbs, a talented baseball player whose career is derailed by a tragic event. It explores themes of ambition, talent, and the corrupting influence of fame and success in the world of professional sports.

Field of Dreams

This fantasy-drama revolves around a farmer who hears a voice telling him to build a baseball field in his cornfield. The film explores themes of redemption, second chances, and the love of the game. It features notable baseball players and captures the essence of America's national pastime.

Friday Night Lights

Set in a small Texas town, this film chronicles the struggles and triumphs of a high school football team and their dedicated coach. It explores the intense pressure faced by the players, the community's obsession with football, and the impact of the sport on their lives.
